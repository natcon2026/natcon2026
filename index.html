About the Conference

The conference “New Vision on the SDGs: Strengthening Resilience & Sustainable Practices and Regulatory Awareness in Healthcare, Management, IT” brings together educators, students, professionals, administrators, and industry leaders to explore how the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) can be translated into measurable, real-world outcomes across three high-impact sectors: healthcare, management, and information technology. At a time when systems are being tested by public health challenges, climate risks, economic uncertainty, and rapid technological change, the conference focuses on a central idea: sustainability is no longer optional—it is a core competency. Resilience, ethical governance, regulatory compliance, and responsible innovation are becoming the backbone of institutions that want to grow while protecting people, data, and the planet.

The conference encourages interdisciplinary research, innovation, and collaboration, fostering meaningful discussions that contribute to societal development and academic excellence.
